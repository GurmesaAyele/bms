Request Requirements

To ensure the safety and efficiency of our blood request process, please have the following information ready:

Medical Information

  • Doctor's prescription or medical order
  • Blood type (if known)
  • Number of units needed
  • Medical condition or reason for transfusion
  • Urgency level and timeline

Hospital Information

  • Hospital or treatment center name
  • Hospital contact information
  • Attending physician details
  • Department or unit information

Patient Information

  • Patient's full name and date of birth
  • Contact information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Insurance information (if applicable)
  • Medical record number
